Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This is a southern recipe for Mac and Cheese that is used commonly throughout America. It is great for events such as barbeques.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Provologne Cheese
  • Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Macaroni
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Cook Pasta in water
  3. Put cheese on pasta
  4. Bake in oven for 15 minutes
