Steak

Description

This is a classic steak recipe that is versatile enought to be used at any event, from a 4th of July get together to a formal date night.

Ingredients

  • Sirloin steak cut
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary leaves

Steps

  1. Blend together Rosemary and Garlic
  2. Grill Steak
  3. After 5 minutes, grill blend with steak
